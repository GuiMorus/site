Who Am I

  • I'm a developer

I began my technical training in Computer Science at Senac São Paulo, where I built a solid foundation in hardware, operating systems, and networks. I work with computer assembly and maintenance, troubleshooting, infrastructure, and technical support, with an understanding of Von Neumann architecture and the internal workings of machines.

During my training, I worked with information security, backup policies, wireless networks, servers, software licensing, and programming fundamentals, which allows me to have a complete view of the IT environment, from hardware to software.

My foundation in development began at Senac, focusing on Web (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), Mobile First, and responsiveness. I evolved in a self-taught way, with references like Gustavo Guanabara, deepening my knowledge in programming logic, Python, and JavaScript.

Today I develop applications with React, Node.js, and REST APIs, as well as exploring Flutter for mobile and Python for automations and intelligent solutions. I focus on performance, code organization, best practices, and user experience, always seeking to evolve within the Full Stack ecosystem.

Design came in as a differentiator and today it is one of my strengths. I work with fundamentals such as visual hierarchy, typography, contrast, color theory, grid and spacing, always focusing on efficient visual communication and UI/UX.

I have experience in creating modern interfaces, visual identities, and conversion-oriented layouts, applying concepts such as color psychology, visual consistency, and storytelling (Golden Circle). My goal is to unite aesthetics and functionality, creating experiences that truly communicate.

Also through Senac São Paulo, I deepened my knowledge in Publicity and Marketing, developing a strategic vision on branding, positioning, and digital communication.

I have knowledge in campaign planning, content creation, social media, graphic materials, and budget management, always thinking about attraction, engagement, and conversion. I understand the language of digital and apply it directly to projects, connecting technology, design, and strategy.
